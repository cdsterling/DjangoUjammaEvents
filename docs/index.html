UJAMMA EVENTS

Ujamma is well known for being one of the seven principles of Kwanzaa. In particular, Ujamma is the principle of Cooperative Economics, whereby community members build and maintain their own businesses and profit from them together.

Ujamma Events connects indepent event spaces with organizations and people who are looking for spaces to host their events without paying the exhorbitant ticketmaster and eventbrite fees!
